Thoughost is now accepting submissions for **KAKUSATSU SHOUJO 4**.

The series continues to focus on anime-sampled cuts built around violence, madness, and cuteness. Genre is open. What matters most is whether the piece carries a strong personal read on that tension.

## Submission notes

- Any genre is welcome
- Anime-style girl vocal sampling remains central to the theme
- Unreleased tracks only
- Demo delivery can happen through the form or by email

For the broader direction of the line, contributors should still use earlier entries in the series as reference points, but new interpretations are encouraged. We want the next chapter to feel continuous without becoming repetitive.
