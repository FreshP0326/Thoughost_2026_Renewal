**BROADCAST** is a smaller publishing lane inside Thoughost.

Some updates are too minor for a full feature page but still worth logging: preview fragments, collaboration notes, schedule nudges, and short production notices. BROADCAST is where those items can live without inflating them into something heavier than they need to be.

## Expect to see

- short release fragments
- collaboration previews
- schedule adjustments
- site-side editorial notes

It should stay compact, fast to update, and easy to browse.
