The site now has a dedicated **NEWS** archive instead of leaving updates only on the front page.

This change gives Thoughost a place to collect label notes, release announcements, and small editorial updates in one timeline. The goal is simple: make each update easier to find later, not just easier to post once.

## What changes

- A full archive page for published news
- Individual article pages for each update
- Shared content source between the homepage and the archive
- A structure that can be maintained with plain Markdown files

## Why we built it this way

Thoughost updates tend to sit between release information, visual notes, and short project announcements. A lightweight Markdown workflow keeps that material close to the site and easy to revise without introducing a CMS.

As the archive grows, it should feel closer to an editorial log than a conventional blog feed: short, direct, and easy to scan.
